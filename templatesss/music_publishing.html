Music Publishing Royalties You Get

Mechanical Streaming Royalties

Mechanical royalties are earned from the reproduction of your music in any format.

Performance Royalties

Performance royalties are earned when your music is played on radio, TV, or live venues.

Sync Royalties

Sync royalties are earned when your music is used in films, TV shows, commercials, and video games.

Neighbouring Rights

Neighbouring rights royalties are earned when your music is played publicly, such as on the radio or in a club.
